Chlorophyll-a is the green pigment in plants and algae that is used for photosynthesis and is a good indicator of the total amount of algae in a lake. Elevated chlorophyll-a concentrations can indicate nutrient enrichment (eutrophication), leading to algal blooms that disrupt the balance of aquatic life. Total phosphorus High levels of total phosphorus in water can come from either wastewater or run-off from agricultural land. Too much phosphorus can encourage the growth of nuisance plants such as algal blooms. Nitrate-nitrogen Nitrate-nitrogen is a very important plant nutrient but because it is highly water soluble, it leaches through soils and into groundwater very easily, particularly after heavy rainfall. Escherichia coli Human contact E. coli (Escherichia coli) is an indicator of the faecal contamination and the presence of disease-causing organisms. Common sources of E.coli bacteria are human wastewater discharges, animal waste, bird droppings and stormwater run-off. E.coli is harmful to human health in high concentrations. {% endcall %} {% endif %} {% endcall %} {% endcall %} {% endcall %} {% call page_container_plain.render() %} {{ divider.render(divider_width="border-b-2") }} {% endcall %} {% call page_container_two_columns.render() %} {% call child_container_sixty_percent_basis.render() %} {% call(content) graph_frame.render(graph_title="The 'current stateâ€™ of lake and river water quality in the catchment.",flyout_id="placeholder9") %} {% if content == 'flyout' %} {% call flyout.render(flyout_id="placeholder9") %} {% call datasource_item.render(link="https://envdata.orc.govt.nz/AQWebPortal/Disclaimer?returnUrl=%2FData",link_title="AQWebPortal") %} Data were obtained from the Otago Regional Council Environmental Data Portal website {% endcall %} {% call datasource_item.render(link="https://www.lawa.org.nz/",link_title="LAWA") %} Data were obtained from the Land Air Water Aotearoa website {% endcall %} {% endcall %} {% endif %} {% if content == 'graph' %} {% call graph_container_large.render() %} {% endcall %} {% endif %} {% endcall %} {% endcall %} {% call child_container_forty_percent_basis.render() %} {% call child_container_full_width.render() %} {% call paragraph.render() %} Monitoring water quality parameters provides a comprehensive understanding of the health of aquatic systems, their impact on human well-being, and the need for sustainable environmental management practices: {% endcall %} {% call text_list.render() %} {% call unorderlist_item.render() %} Monitoring nitrate levels helps manage agricultural practices to minimise pollution and protect water resources;
